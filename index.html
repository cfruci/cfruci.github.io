About Me

I live in Denver with my beautiful wife and sons. We spend a lot of time outside in the backyard and on walks around our neighborhood. We laugh a lot. We're happy and blessed.

I've had a winding, yet fulfilling career thus far. After graduating from Vanderbilt in 2013, I moved to Colorado with a few of my closest friends. We played progressive rock music together in a band called Archive Nights. It was a special time of life.

Our Clock Ocean - EP holds a particularly special place in my heart. Check it out here. Every once in awhile, I'll listen back to this album and get emotional. Songs like "Why I" and "Clock Ocean," especially, feel like they were gifted to us.

Around the time Archive Nights broke up, I joined the DaVita "Village" in 2015 as a Corporate Strategy Analyst. Our team - "The Scouts" - was full of thoughtful, intelligent, and kind people. I grew a lot personally and professionally here. And we had a lot of fun.

I left DaVita in 2018 to give music another shot. My bandmate Will and I knew we'd regret it if we didn't give everything we had to a new and exciting project,Compass + Cavern. Over the next 7 years, we took risks and grew into better performers. We learned how to be business owners and turn a creative endeavor into a profitable enterprise. I'm so proud of what we accomplished.

We fought hard through COVID, got married to our spouses, and became parents. In 2025, shortly after releasing a full-length album, we entered an indefinite hiatus but excited to pursue other projects. We're still performing together in a rock Disney cover band called The Man Cubs with three other close friends. It's a thrill.

After leaving DaVita, I started a freelance content writing business to pay the bills. I started off making $40/post for articles that would take me 4-5 hours to write. Within two years, I was supporting 10-12 businesses at a time and providing a much wider range of content and growth marketing services. I started studying the art of persuasion and craft of writing much more closely. And under the guidance of several key marketers in my life, I've expanded into a marketing generalist.

Guidelines

Contributing a stylesheet to Style Stage means you agree to abide by our full guidelines.

TL;DR

All submissions will be autoprefixed and prepended with the CC BY-NC-SA license as well as attribution using the metadata you provide. You may use any build setup you prefer, but the final submission should be the compiled, unminified CSS. You retain the copyright to original graphics and must ensure all graphics used are appropriately licensed. All asset links, including fonts, must be absolute to external resources. Stylesheets will be saved into the Github repo, and detected changes that violate the guidelines are cause for removal.

Ensure your design is responsive, and that it passes accessible contrast (we'll be using aXe to verify). Animations should be removed via `prefers-reduced-motion`. Cutting-edge techniques should come with a fallback if needed to not severely impact the user experience. No content may be permanently hidden, and hidden items must come with an accessible viewing technique. Page load time should not exceed 3 seconds.

Most importantly - have fun and learn something new! Check out the resources for tips and inspiration.

Contribute

All who enjoy the craft of writing CSS are welcome to contribute!

By participating as a contributor, your work will be shared with your provided attribution as long as Style Stage is online, your stylesheet link and any asset links remain valid, and all contributor guidelines are adhered to.

Steps to Contribute

  1. Download the source files listed below to use as a reference to build your stylesheet, or start from the Sass template.
  2. Host your completed stylesheet at a public URL, and ensure all asset links are absolute URLs to external resources.
  3. Create a pull request to add your information as a unique .json file to: src/_data/styles. The schema is detailed in the repo README, and you can review the FAQ on creating a pull request.
  4. If your contribution abides by the previously listed guidelines, your submission will be added!
